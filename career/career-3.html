Project Coordinator

Date Posted - 01/06/2024

Location - Hybrid

Employment Type - Full-Time

Salary – Negotiable

Job Description

As a Project Coordinator, you will play a critical role in supporting project management activities and ensuring the successful execution of various projects. You will work closely with project managers, team members, and stakeholders to coordinate tasks, manage schedules, and facilitate communication. This role requires strong organizational skills, the ability to multitask, and a keen eye for detail.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist project managers in planning, executing, and closing projects.
  • Coordinate project tasks and activities, resources, and information ensuring they are completed on time and within scope.
  • Prepare and maintain project documentation, including plans, schedules, and reports.
  • Track project progress, identify potential issues, and report them to the project manager.
  • Serve as a point of contact for project teams, providing timely updates and facilitating effective communication.
  • Assist in identifying project risks and developing mitigation strategies.
  • Schedule and organize project meetings, prepare agendas, and document minutes.
  • Provide administrative support to project managers and team members as needed.
  • Ensure projects adhere to frameworks and all documentation is maintained appropriately.

Qualifications

  • Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Project Management, or a related field.
  • Proven experience as a Project Coordinator or similar role.
  • Familiarity with project management methodologies and tools.
  • Strong organizational and multitasking skills.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities.
  • Proficiency in MS Office and project management software (e.g., Slack, discord, Jira).
  • Ability to work effectively in a team-oriented environment.

Preferred Skills

  • Experience with Agile and Scrum methodologies.
  • Certification in project management (e.g., CAPM, PMP) is a plus.
  • Familiarity with software development projects.
  • Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail.

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary and benefits package.
  • Opportunities for professional development and career growth.
  • A collaborative and innovative work environment.
  • Flexible working hours and the possibility of remote work.
  • The chance to work on exciting projects with a talented and passionate team.
